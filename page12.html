Benefits and Customers

Presenter: Jose Raphael R. Lawas

Expected Benefits

Improved Emergency Response Time

Faster access to critical medical information for first responders and medical professionals

Enhanced Patient Safety

Reduced risk of inappropriate treatment due to readily available medical history

Increased Efficiency in Emergency Care

Streamlined information access improves workflow for healthcare providers

Peace of Mind for Users

Provides individuals with a sense of security knowing their medical information is accessible in emergencies

Key Stakeholders

End Users (Individuals)

People who will use the emergency medical alert system and benefit from quick access to their medical information

Medical Professionals

Doctors, paramedics, and other healthcare providers who will access medical information during emergencies

Hospitals & Emergency Services

Healthcare facilities and emergency response teams that will integrate the system into their workflows

Emergency Contacts

Family members or designated contacts who will be notified during emergencies

Project Team

The team responsible for developing and implementing the MediAlert system

PMO Manager

Oversees the project and ensures alignment with organizational objectives

Final Customers

Individuals & Medical Professionals

The ultimate customers who will use the MediAlert system and rely on it during emergency situations

The MediAlert system delivers tangible benefits to both users and medical professionals by providing crucial information when seconds count. Our affordable solution addresses a critical need while creating a safer healthcare environment.
