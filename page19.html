Key Takeaways & Q&A

Presenter: Jose Raphael R. Lawas

Key Takeaways

Addressing a Critical Need

MediAlert provides immediate access to critical medical information during emergencies, potentially saving lives through faster and more appropriate treatment.

Security & Privacy By Design

Our solution incorporates robust security measures and compliance with data privacy regulations to protect sensitive medical information.

Broad Accessibility

The affordable and user-friendly design ensures accessibility to all individuals regardless of technological proficiency or socioeconomic status.

Future-Ready Platform

MediAlert establishes the foundation for future enhancements including mobile applications, hospital system integration, and expanded functionality.

Q&A Session

How is patient data secured?

MediAlert employs end-to-end encryption, secure authentication protocols, and role-based access controls to protect sensitive medical information.

What is the implementation timeline?

Initial launch in April 2025, with pilot deployment followed by full rollout. Future enhancements are planned over the subsequent 24 months.

What is the total budget requirement?

Total project budget is PHP 1,720,900 with a contingency reserve of PHP 180,900 (10%).

MediAlert represents not just a technological solution, but a transformative approach to emergency medical care that can make a significant difference in treatment outcomes.
