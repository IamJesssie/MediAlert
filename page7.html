Procurement Management

Given the project's scope as a student-led initiative, no significant external procurement is anticipated. If needed in future phases, we will follow this process:

1

Identify Procurement Needs

Project team identifies need for external goods or services

2

Meet with Department

Consult with department to check documentation requirements

3

Find Vendor

Identify and evaluate potential vendors based on requirements

4

Make an Offer

Present requirements and negotiate terms with selected vendor

5

Create an Agreement

Formalize the partnership with appropriate documentation

Our procurement approach follows university guidelines to ensure compliance while managing resources efficiently.

Effective communication is critical to our project success. Our framework ensures all stakeholders remain informed and engaged throughout the project lifecycle.
