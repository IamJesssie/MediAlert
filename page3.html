Project Charter & Overview

Presenter: Jose Raphael R. Lawas

Problem Statement

Lack of immediate access to critical medical information during emergencies leads to delayed or inappropriate medical treatment, potentially worsening patient outcomes.

Purpose

To develop and implement an affordable emergency medical alert system that uses QR codes on bracelets to provide immediate access to an individual's medical history for first responders.

Business Case

Faster access to medical history can lead to more effective treatment, potentially saving lives. The project aligns with the organization's commitment to innovation and social responsibility.

Goals & Metrics

System Response: Load time under 2 seconds

User Registration: 10,000 users in 6 months

System Uptime: 99.9% availability

User Satisfaction: Average rating of 4/5 stars

Expected Deliverables

Medical alert website

QR code management system

Secure user database

Emergency alert notifications

Project documentation

Deployed system

"In emergency situations, seconds count. MediAlert aims to provide immediate access to critical medical information when it matters most."
