Risk Management

Presenter: Joshua Noel D. Lo

Key Project Risks

Data Security Breach

High

Unauthorized access to sensitive medical information

Mitigation: Strong encryption, access controls, regular security audits

User Privacy Concerns

High

Public apprehension about privacy and security of data

Mitigation: Regulatory compliance, transparent privacy policies

Website Downtime

High

System unavailability during critical emergency situations

Mitigation: Redundant systems, failover capabilities, backups

Low User Adoption

Medium

Lack of user acceptance and registration

Mitigation: User-friendly design, awareness campaigns

Inaccurate Medical Information

Medium

Users provide incomplete or outdated medical data

Mitigation: Clear instructions, validation checks, disclaimers

Risk Management Methodology

Identification

Brainstorming, expert interviews, documentation review

Assessment

Evaluate likelihood and impact of each identified risk

Response

Develop strategies: avoid, mitigate, transfer, or accept

Monitoring

Continuously track risks and adjust strategies as needed
