Risk Response Strategies

Avoid

Eliminate the threat by removing the cause. For example, removing third-party integrations that pose significant security risks.

Mitigate

Reduce probability or impact to an acceptable threshold. For example, implementing robust security measures to mitigate data breach risks.

Transfer

Shift impact and ownership to a third party. For example, using cloud service providers with robust uptime guarantees.

Accept

Acknowledge risk without taking action, typically for low-impact or highly unlikely risks. Active acceptance may include contingency reserves.
